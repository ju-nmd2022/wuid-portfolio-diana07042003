In the next paragraphs I am going to present you some information about Jonkoping city and what you can do in this beautiful town.

What can you do here?

The university in Jonkoping, which is among the top universities in Sweden, is the reason for the city's fame. In spite of this knowledge, Jonkping dazzles with the breath-taking lakes. Although Jonkoping is a typical city, there are plenty of things to do there. For instance, there are several magnificent parks where you can spend your leisure time unwinding. Additionally, there are frequently events taking place, such holidays, Halloween, the start of the school year, and so forth.

What are the best places to go out?

As a student, Akademian is the greatest location to go when it comes to parting. Despite the low prices, you may enjoy yourself dancing and singing along with the students from Jonkoping University, a great place to meet people and have some special moments. There are numerous parks where you can rest if you prefer the peace and quiet of nature, such as Radhusparken, Knektaparken, Lassaretsparken, etc.

What are the best places to eat?

There are several pubs and restaurants in Jonkoping where you may have a wonderful and delicious meal. The tastiest burgers in town can be found at Bada Bing Burgers. Texas Longhorn is a favorite among those who enjoy really spicy food, while La Vue is a great place to enjoy a cup of coffee while admiring the lake.
